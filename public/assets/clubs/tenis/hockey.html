Player Biography

Jack Eichel, born October 28, 1996, is an American professional ice hockey center and captain of the Vegas Golden Knights of the National Hockey League (NHL). Eichel was selected second overall in the 2015 NHL Entry Draft by the Buffalo Sabres.

Before the draft, he was described as a "new-generation power forward, combining size, speed, and skill." He is known for his incredible puck-handling skills and a powerful shot.
